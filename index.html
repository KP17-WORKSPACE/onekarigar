Frequently Asked Questions

Got questions? We've got answers

How does OneKarigar verify its karigars?

All karigars undergo thorough background checks, skill verification tests, and customer reviews before being onboarded. We also provide ongoing training and quality assessments.

What is the pricing model?

We provide 100% transparent pricing with detailed quotations. You pay based on project milestones. No hidden costs, no surprises. Everything is documented clearly.

What is covered under the warranty?

Our written warranty covers workmanship defects, material issues, and installation problems for a specified period (varies by service). We provide free repairs for covered issues.

Which cities do you serve?

We currently serve 10+ cities across India, primarily focusing on Tier-2 and Tier-3 cities. Contact us to check if we're available in your area.

How long does a typical project take?

Project duration varies based on scope and complexity. We provide a detailed timeline during quotation. Our architect supervision ensures projects stay on schedule.

Can I get a free consultation?

Yes! We offer free consultations where we visit your site, understand your requirements, and provide a detailed quotation at no cost.
