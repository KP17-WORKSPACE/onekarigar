What is OneKarigar?

OneKarigar is a tech-enabled skilled services platform that connects customers with verified construction and interior karigars under one trusted brand.

Problems We Solve

  • No fixed pricing or transparency in costs
  • No bills, receipts, or warranty on work done
  • Poor workmanship and unprofessional behavior
  • Zero accountability after project completion
  • Customers managing multiple unreliable vendors

OneKarigar Solution

  • Single point of contact for all services

  • 100% transparent pricing with detailed quotations
  • Architect-led supervision for quality assurance
  • Written warranty and dedicated after-service support
